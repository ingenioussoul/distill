The capture

Two ways in. One swipe apart.

The capture has to be faster than the friction is forgettable. No form to fill — just the thing in front of you, and your voice one diagonal away.

01
Point & shoot
Open straight into the viewfinder. One photo of the thing that stopped you — the default, the fastest, the one you'll actually reach for.
02 · swipe ↗
Hold & speak
A diagonal swipe brings audio up over the camera. Hold, say what caught your eye, release. For friction you feel but can't photograph.
03
Type — if you must
Writing is the fallback, never the front door. One line to name what you saw or said. Then it's out of your head and into the pattern.
